Privacy Policy

Heartbot, managed, developed, maintained, and run by "The Poisoned Cookies" developers.

Understanding our privacy policy helps you know your rights within our services better.

Your Payment Info

We offer payments in crypto and using credit card.

Crypto payments are made directly by sending funds to a crypto address we provide. On our backend, we do not process any of your identity related to that payment, such as your address or other personal details.

The payment address is provided by NOWPayments, which proxies your payment by nature, so no direct payment will be visible on the blockchain.

However, when you request a refund, we will retrieve your address via NOWPayments to return the funds.

Credit card, Google Pay, and other payments are handled by Paddle. On our side, we mainly process your payment status, but might access necessary information which is processed and saved by Paddle, not directly by us.

Your last order ID and last purchase info like status and date are linked to your user ID and stored permanently by nature.

You can request removal of this data by opening a support ticket. Removal from Paddle and our system might take 1–7 business days.

Your Online Identity

Your email is linked to your account permanently unless you delete your account.

Your IP address is not linked to your account but is processed for security and abuse prevention.

We do not use tracking or advertising cookies, only session cookies.

We do not use any analytics to track your behavior.

Your Data Using Our Adbotting

Your messages are visible to admins to ensure compliance with our Terms of Service. Your last 24 hours of messages are stored temporarily to prevent abuse and then permanently deleted.

Chats you join using bots are controlled by you. When you exit a chat or drop an instance, the chat record is deleted entirely and permanently.

Bots leave any chat you remove. Chats and bots are not visible or accessible to our staff.

You must be 13 years or older to use our services.

Policy Updates

This policy may be updated from time to time. You will be notified of updates via email.
